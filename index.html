    How to play

    1. Read the customer order at the top.
    2. Work through the steps like a bartender: glass → pour → mix → garnish.
    3. Add ingredients and dial in each amount in ml (some use dashes/leaves). Watch them pour in.
    4. Pick a method and a garnish, then Serve.
    5. Earn up to 3 stars per drink. Pours within a small tolerance still count!
    6. Basic mode auto-picks the glass & method; Advanced leaves it all to you.
